About

Hi, I'm Timothy Mwiti.

I am a rising junior at Northwestern University. I major in Computer Science. My career goal is to eventually start my own software development company in my home country, Kenya.

When not drowned in school work, I work as a research assistant/software developer at Assistant Professor Marcelo Worsley's TIILT Lab. Our research is centered around using multimodal technology to understand and improve on how students learn in complex environments.

Some of my hobbies include cycling, photography, reading and playing video games. Main interests include entrepreneurship and tech. I am a lover of sports. I enjoy watching soccer (Manchester United fan) as well as the NBA and College Football. I'll have a good time watching any kind of sport.

Over time I have learnt that one of the most effective way to grow is to talk to people and hear their views. Therefore, I enjoy meeting new people and networking. Feel free to reach out through the means on the contact page or through my social media below.
